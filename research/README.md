# Greyline research corpus

This directory is the consolidated, citation-backed research that grounds Greyline's product direction. It replaces the scattered prior artifacts (the root `GRAY_MAN_RESEARCH.md`, the `.research/` home-page study) with one indexed corpus. Every report is generated from primary-source web research (2023–2026) and grades its own claims; where a source is weak it says so.

> **Provenance & honesty.** These reports synthesize peer-reviewed papers, official docs/standards, active OSS, and real product pages. Claims are kept faithful to their sources and cited with URLs. The gray-man report in particular **grades every claim** (Validated / Contested / Folklore) because the document it replaces was blog-sourced and contained fabricated statistics — see its "Myths we removed" section.
